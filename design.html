OUR DESIGN

Outer Aperture

The outer section of DEMI is two hinging doors. The first is a quarter circle. In our protoype, this door has a polycarbonate cover. In practice this would be aluminum as with all other parts. The second door is a flat flap that can move within the circular door while minimizing gaps.

Upon approaching a piece of junk, both doors open. Then once the object is in grabbing range, the doors close, encapsulating the object.

Inner Aperture

The inner section of DEMI takes inspriation from a garage door. Two motors, one on each side work to raise and lower the two sides of the door.

When an object becomes contained by the outer doors, the inner doors roll open. Then the flat door rotates within the circular door and pushes the object through the doors and into the final storage area.
